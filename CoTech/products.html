Platforms Built for Real Problems

Four flagship products — each purpose-built for a specific industry, each production-proven at scale across multiple continents.

UniERP

University ERP

A comprehensive enterprise platform covering every aspect of university operations — from admissions to accreditation, in a single, unified system.

  • Admissions, Enrolment & Student Lifecycle Management
  • Academics, Timetable, Attendance & Exams
  • Finance, Budgeting, Fees & Accounts
  • HRMS, Payroll, Leave & Appraisals
  • Library, Hostel, Transport & Building
  • RBAC, Multi-Tenant, Audit Logs & Accreditation
MediCore

Healthcare Information System

A modular, interoperable HIS platform for hospitals and clinics that integrates clinical workflows, patient records, billing, and compliance reporting with full audit trails.

  • Electronic Medical Records (EMR/EHR)
  • OPD, IPD, Emergency & Ward Management
  • Pharmacy, Laboratory & Radiology
  • Insurance, TPA & Claims Processing
  • Nurse stations and bedside terminals
  • HL7 FHIR-compatible API layer
PulseAnalytics

AI Business Intelligence

Real-time analytics and AI-powered insights connecting your operational data with strategic decision-making. Supports natural language queries, automated reports, and predictive modelling.

  • Unified data warehouse with real-time connectors
  • Predictive forecasting and trend detection
  • Custom KPI dashboards per role and department
  • Natural language query interface (NLP)
  • Automated report scheduling and distribution
  • Embeddable analytics widgets
FlowCRM

Enterprise CRM Platform

A full-cycle CRM for complex sales organizations — multi-pipeline management, AI lead scoring, workflow automation, and a native mobile app that keeps your team productive on the move.

  • Multi-pipeline and stage-based deal management
  • AI lead scoring, routing, and follow-up automation
  • Email, SMS & WhatsApp communication hub
  • Customer success, renewals and upsell workflows
  • Deep analytics: conversion, velocity, win rate
  • Native iOS and Android application
